About Us

Reasons to choose

‘Homestyles’ is a boutique design firm, involved exceptionally qualified designers that work cooperatively to make enlivened and interesting interior conditions. Our wide scope of demographic; private to senior living, property holder to corporate clients, all craving to hoist their spaces and draw in with their surroundings.

We do this since we give it a second thought. Our enthusiasm is to make spaces that mirror your vision and bring bliss and solace. We care for individuals by making spaces where they feel comfortable, yet work perfectly. With more than 20 years of design involvement, we have the expertise, learning, and scrupulousness to comfort your psyche through the design procedure.

Our Services

What We Do?

Interior Design

Every home is a unique expression of its homeowners. We ensure your house design is in keeping with your distinct tastes. Everything, from creating furniture to choosing furnishings and lighting, is meant to reflect your sense of design.

Design Consultancy

If you need explicit comments on the aesthetics and functionality of your products, projects and services, or if you are seeking to work with the World’s top designers, the A’ Design Award offers Design Consultancy Service in collab with World Design Consortium.

Residential Design

Across the range of services provided — including development, occupational and investment advice — our team ensures that customized solutions are aligned to the key business objectives of our clients.

Commercial Design

A well designed work environment can support productivity, inspire creativity or encourage teamwork. Whether you’re replacing outdated fixtures and furniture or rearranging to accommodate your growing business, long lasting designs that truly work for your business.

Hospitality Design

Owners and operators of hospitality facilities, including hotels, luxury resorts, golf and country clubs, timeshare communities and conference centers, count on HVS Design for their design expertise in new construction, renovation and restoration projects.

Co-working Space Design

Modern workspaces must be adaptable and ergonomically sound. Cast iron, plywood and hanging plants are also requisite. Dispatching from creative companies and desirable desk spaces, we track down the designers responding to our evolving working behaviours.
